FicFetch is an online story downloader.

This is a client-side app, meaning it costs me nothing to run, and can be hosted for free on my GitHub.

Check out the support link for info on what formats and sites are currently available.

Created with ❤ for the community over at /r/HPFanFiction by Adam Franco.

Support Information

I will be periodically adding support for different websites and formats, but there is no real time-frame for updates or fixes.

If you are experiencing any issues downloading a story, or have a suggestion for future updates to dandy, please send an email to support@ficfetch.xyz. I will try to provide help as often as I can.
